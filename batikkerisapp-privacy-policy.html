Privacy Policy - Siap Berbatik

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Siap Berbatik application developed and managed by Badan Kesatuan Bangsa dan Politik (Kesbangpol) Kabupaten Deli Serdang. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect information within the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless it is voluntarily submitted by the user for specific services, such as:

  • Submission of research recommendation requests (BATIK KERIS)
  • Organizational registration data (BATIK PERTAMAS)
  • Community reports and input (BATIK WARDIMOR)

All collected data is used solely for the intended service purposes and in accordance with applicable regulations.

2. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions for functionality:

  • Internet Access: To send and retrieve data from our secure servers.
  • Storage Access: To allow users to upload relevant documents or files (e.g., research proposals, organizational documents).

3. Use of Information

Any submitted information is used exclusively to process public service requests within the app. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. All information remains within the scope of government service and is treated confidentially.

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect the data submitted through this app, including encryption, secure connections (HTTPS), and role-based access control for administrators.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or app functionality. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us via GitHub: r4dioz88
